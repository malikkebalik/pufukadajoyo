Aha plans free premieres for its upcoming web series

Published on May 3, 2023 11:52 pm IST


Noted streaming service Aha and famous production house People Media Factory have teamed up for a web series called Newsense. Actor Navdeep and Bindu Madhavi are the lead actors in this political drama series.

The latest to know is that Aha has come up with a plan to premiere the season 1 of the series for free in 5 cities in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. The special screening details are as follows.

May 8 – Vijayawada
May 9 – Vizag and Rajahmundry
May 10 – Tirupathi
May 11 – Hyderabad

The interesting part is Navdeep and Bindu Madhavi will watch all these shows with the public. Newsense season 1 will be premiered on Aha on May 12, 2023. Created and directed by Sri Prawin Kumar, the series has notable actors in key roles. Suresh Bobbili is the music director for this series. Keep browsing this space for more OTT updates.
