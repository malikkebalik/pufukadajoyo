At a very tender age, Camren Bicondova began training at a local dance studio. When was 11, she became an “Elite Protégé” for the international traveling dance convention ‘The PULSE on Tour’. As a member of the convention, she got the privilege of traveling all over the country and assisting some of the nation’s top choreographers and dance teachers.

In 2011, she first appeared on television in an episode of ‘Shake It Up’. After her TV debut, she came to mainstream prominence only a year later when she appeared in the dance-drama flick ‘Battlefield America’. She also participated in the 7th season of ‘America’s Best Dance Crew’ where she was declared as the runner-up with her all-girl dance group “8 Flavahz.”

She was also featured in Ciara’s music video “Got Me Good”. In 2013, she was cast in the Canadian slasher film ‘Girl House’. The following year, she played the role of Selina Kyle / Catwoman in the TV series ‘Gotham’. In 2014, she again got an opportunity to appear in Krewella’s music video “Enjoy the Ride”.

As a backup dancer, she has appeared in Dana Vaughns’ music video “Underneath.” She is also involved in many charitable activities and associated with non-profit organizations, such as The USO, Global Citizen Festival, NOH8 Campaign, and North Shore Animal League America.
