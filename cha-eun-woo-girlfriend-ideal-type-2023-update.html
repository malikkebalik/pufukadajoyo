Who is Cha Eun Woo? 

Cha Eun Woo is a famous South Korean singer, rising actor, and model. He is active as a member of the boy group, Astro.

Aside from his group activities, Eun Woo getting a lot of attention for his acting in recent years. Eun Woo made his acting debut in the 2016 web drama, My Romance Some Recipe. 

Is Cha Eun Woo pure Korean? Why did he change his name? 

Despite his popularity, many might not know this, but Cha Eun Woo is not his real name. In fact, this dashing actor was born Lee Dong Min in Gunpo, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea.

As to why he changed his name, Cha, along with his fellow Astro members, appeared in the drama titled “To Be Continued.”

There he portrayed the character named “Cha Eun Woo.” The actor got huge exposure thanks to the drama, and everyone around him simply got used to calling him by his character’s name.

Likewise, it is not unusual for Korean celebrities to have a stage name, and considering all that, he finally changed his stage name to Cha Eun Woo. 

On the other hand, Eun Woo has not mentioned much when it comes to his family and their whereabouts. As for his siblings, Cha has a younger brother who is studying in China at the moment. 

Education and Childhood 

Considered the smartest member of Astro, young Eun Woo went to Neungnae Elementary School and then enrolled at the Suri Middle School and Suri High School.

He also studied at an elementary school in the Philippines called Tagaytay to learn English. 

Upon graduating from high school, Eun Woo was then admitted to the Hanlim Multi Art School, where he studied broadcasting and entertainment.

He was a class president and captain of the basketball team in his senior years. Eun Woo finally graduated in 2016. 

Since then, he attended Sungkyungkwan University, where he was accepted as a significant actor back in November 2015.

Likewise, Cha Eun Woo’s worth is more than his other Astro members. He earned such hefty income thanks to his solo acting gigs and projects as MC.

Since he has the ideal Korean standard face, Cha is widely popular in his home country. 

Not to mention, he has endorsed South Korean oven-roasted chicken franchises Goobne Chicken, LizK, and Tik Tok. At the moment, he is considered the King of CF.

Trainee Days and Debut as Astro member

Despite his dashing looks, Cha Eun Woo never imagined being an actor or singer in the first place. Not to mention, his mother always reminded him not to be vain of his looks.

But during his Hanlim days, young Eun Woo participated in the KBS’s high-school quiz program called “Golden Bell Challenge!” 

Thanks to his good looks, Eun Woo was noticed by an idol agent and later scouted by a Fantagio manager during a school festival.

Even though he refused initially, the talent hunters persisted and eventually persuaded his parents to let him be a trainee. 

Similarly, in 2013, Eun Woo took part in ‘Mr. Pizza’s iTeen Audition for Promotional Model’ with band member Moon Bin.

A few months later, young Cha starred in the KBS news segment, Hallyu Star & Gangnam Style: Trainee Interview’ again with Moon Bin, Rocky, and Jinjin. 

Finally, Cha Eun Woo made his debut as an Astro on February 23, 2016, with their mini-album, Spring Up. Then he became the MC of the show, Show! Music Core alongside Kim Sae Ron and Lee Soo Min. He hosted the show from 2016 to 2018. 

After debuting as a singer, Eun Woo was cast in the KBS2 drama, Hit the Top, followed by another web drama, Sweet Revenge.

The following year, he starred in a web drama titled, Top Management. That year he starred in his first leading role in the JTBC romantic comedy series Gangnam Beauty. The success of the series catapulted Eun Woo’s career to a new height.
