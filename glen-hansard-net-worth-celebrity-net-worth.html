What is Glen Hansard's Net Worth?

Glen Hansard is an Irish musician and actor who has a net worth of $5 million. Born in Ballymun, Dublin, Ireland, in 1970, Glen Hansard formed the band The Frames in 1990. The group has released several albums between 1991 and 2010, including "Another Love Song", "Fitzcarraldo", "Dance the Devil", "Breadcrumb Trail", and "Burn the Maps". A year after forming the band, Hansard starred in the award-winning movie "The Commitments", earning international acclaim for his performance. He has since appeared in several other films, TV shows, and documentaries, including 2007's "Once", 2008's "Austin City Limits", 2011's "Ballymun Lullaby", "The Simpsons", "Parenthood", and 2013's "In the Deep Shade". Hansard released his first album without his original band members in 2006.

"The Swell Season" features collaborations with several other artists, including Marja Tuhkanen and Marketa Irglova, whom he was previously linked to romantically. He later released his solo album, "Rhythm and Repose", in 2012, which climbed to number 21 on the U.S. top 200 and topped the Irish Independent Albums chart. Hansard performed at the funeral of Eunice Kennedy Shriver. He has been involved in several charity projects, including helping raise funds for the Simon Community. He toured with Eddie Vedder and performed at the Metropolitan Museum of the Art in New York City.
