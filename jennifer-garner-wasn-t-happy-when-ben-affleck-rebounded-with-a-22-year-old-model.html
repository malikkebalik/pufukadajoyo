Jennifer Garner Wasn't 'Happy' When Ben Affleck Rebounded With a 22-Year-Old Model

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

For years, Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck were Hollywood’s “it” couple. Unfortunately, they couldn’t make their romance last. After a decade together, they called it quits on their romance in 2015 before finalizing their divorce in 2018. Before finalizing the divorce, Affleck was spotted with 22-year-old Playboy model Shauna Sexton. Here’s how Garner allegedly felt about Affleck dating the young model.

Jennifer Garner allegedly wasn’t ‘happy’ when Ben Affleck dated a 22-year-old model after their split

Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck retained a friendship after their split. The couple was married from 2005 to 2018, though they split in 2015. They share three children and vowed to remain close for the good of the kids. While Garner and Affleck have a close relationship in 2024, it seems Affleck’s choice of women might’ve bothered Garner before they finalized their divorce.

Affleck and 22-year-old Playboy model Shauna Sexton were spotted together in August 2018 before splitting up two months later. A source told People at the time that Garner wasn’t “happy” about the romance, but she was more concerned about Affleck maintaining his sobriety and parenting their children.

“Is she happy about him hanging out with a very young Playboy model? Certainly not,” a source said. “But her concern right now is not who he is dating. What she ultimately cares about is his sobriety and whether she can rely on him to co-parent.”

“He is happy being able to date who he wants and not feel tied down to a serious relationship,” another source said. “… Ben likes a woman with brains and beauty, but he also likes to be free to do what he wants.”

Affleck and Sexton broke up so he could focus on more critical aspects of his life. “He enjoyed being with her but is working on himself, and being together with her at this time is not something that works,” a friend told People. “He will date in the future, but for now, his focus is on his sobriety, family, and his next project.”

A source said that Shauna Sexton had ‘no respect’ for him or his recovery

Ben Affleck entered rehab for alcohol addiction on Aug. 22, 2018 — a week after he was spotted out to dinner with Shauna Sexton. A source told Page Six in 2018 that they didn’t believe Sexton had any “respect” for Affleck or his sobriety, given how she loved to party.

“At the end of the day, in order to be in recovery, [Affleck] needs to be by himself and taking care of his family,” the source shared. “He’s being used by this girl. Anyone who drinks the way she drinks and is out there publicizing it and their relationship has no respect for him or his family.”

Sexton defended herself on social media after the allegations. She admitted to making “mistakes” on Instagram but claimed they don’t define her. “Do these mistakes mean I would disrespect someone’s hard-earned sobriety by drinking with them or in front of them? Hell no,” she wrote.

Jennifer Garner allegedly worries about Ben Affleck’s health amid Jennifer Lopez divorce rumors

Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez are going through marital issues in 2024, and Jennifer Garner is allegedly worried about Affleck’s health. Garner helped Affleck with his alcohol addiction in the past. Above all else, she wants him to remain healthy and happy despite whatever happens in his personal life.

“Jen can’t stand by and do nothing,” a source told OK! Magazine. “That’s not in her nature. She has dropped everything to make sure Ben doesn’t spiral out of control again. At the end of the day, Ben is still the father of their children, and she will help him however she can.”
