This Easy Ariana Grande Halloween Costume Is For The Cat Lovers Out There PHOTOS

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Still stuck on what do dress up as for Halloween? If you were running out of ideas and just going to default on cat ears, why not kick it up a notch and check out this Ariana Grande Halloween costume idea? You only need a few pieces beyond the cat ears to pull it off!

Whether Grande is rocking her natural curly locks or looking all kinds of glam on the set of Scream Queens, the young superstar has proven herself a style icon. She takes on any stage with confidence, poise, and power, and isn't afraid to take fun fashion risks during performances.

Another reason to dress up as Ariana Grande for Halloween? The star is all about spreading good vibes and body positivity! She's also not afraid to tackle topics like misogyny and shuts it down in the best possible way.

If you love Grande but are still feeling upset about the whole #donutgate incident, I say it's time to forgive and move on. The singer posted a heartfelt apology for her less than awesome donut licking behavior, and I think that's all the more reason to celebrate this sexy star! Plus, the entire costume is super cute and bound to get lots of compliments.

Grab your cat ears and the pieces below to channel this mega-talented babe.

1. Cat Ears

Because duh.

(Lace Cat Ears, $8.99, Choies.com)

(Boots, $59.99, H&M)

Grande keeps it simple with a chic black choker.
