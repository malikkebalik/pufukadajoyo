What is Tom Verica's Net Worth?

Tom Verica is an American actor, director, and producer who has a net worth of $3 million. Tom Verica was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in May 1964. Verica is best known for playing the role of Jack Pryor in the TV series American Dreams. His first role came on the television series soap opera All My Children in 1987. His debut feature film role came in the 1990 movie Die Hard 2. In 1991 Verica starred as Bill Castroverde in the TV series L.A. Law. From 1995 to 1996 he starred as Mark Merrill in the series C.P.W.

He starred as Jake Sullivan in the television series The Naked Truth from 1997 to 1998. Tom starred as Kyle Moran in the TV series Providence in 1999. From 2002 to 2005 he starred as Jack Pryor in the TV series American Dreams. Verica starred as Ed Nielson in the series The Nine from 2006 to 2007. Starting in 2014 he starred as Sam Keating in the ABC TV series How to Get Away with Murder. He has also starred in the movies Red Dragon, Murder by Numbers, Flags of Our Fathers, and Zodiac. Verica was nominated for a Young Artist Award in 2004.
