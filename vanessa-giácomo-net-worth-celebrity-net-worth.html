Vanessa Giacomo Net Worth:Vanessa Giacomo is a Brazilian actress who has a net worth of $10 million. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, in 1983, Vanessa Giacomo made her on-screen and television debut in 2004's "Cabocla" as Zuca when she was 20 years old. Two years later, she appeared as Maria in "Canta Maria" and as Juliana in "Sinha Moca". That same year, she starred as Simone in "The Twelve Labours". Giacomo had two appearances in 2007: "Duas Caras" as Luciana and "Amazonia: De Galvez a Chico Mendes" as Ilza. She went on to appear in "A Ilha dos Escravos", "Pariso", "Jean Charles", O Menino da Porteira", "Caras & Bocas", Escrito nas Estrelas", "Morde & Assopra", "A Novela das 8", "Gabriela", "Amor a Vida", and "Imperio". Giacomo won the Most Promising Actress Award for "Cabocla" at the 2005 Premio Contigo in Brazil. Two years later, she won the same award at the Premio Contigo de Cinema for 2006's "Canta Maria". While on the set of "Cabocla", Giacomo met her husband, fellow actor Daniel de Oliveira. The two married in 2004 and she gave birth to their first child, a son, in early 2008. Giacomo and her husband then filmed "O Menino da Porteira" together. They eventually filed for divorce in 2012 after eight years together.
